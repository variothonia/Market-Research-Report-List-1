<p><strong>The Night Vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night vision goggles market primarily comprises two types: image intensifier and thermal imaging. Image intensifier goggles amplify low-light conditions by enhancing visible light, making them effective for detecting objects in darkness. In contrast, thermal imaging goggles detect heat emitted by objects, allowing users to see in complete darkness and through obstructions like smoke or fog. Both technologies serve critical roles in military, law enforcement, and outdoor activities, each offering unique advantages based on environmental conditions and operational needs.</p></p>
