<p><p>LIMS (Laboratory Information Management Systems) are categorized into cloud-based and on-premise markets. Cloud-based LIMS offer flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access data remotely and reducing the need for extensive hardware and IT management. This model is ideal for labs seeking cost-effective solutions and rapid deployment. In contrast, on-premise LIMS are installed locally and provide greater control over data security and compliance, making them suitable for organizations with strict regulatory requirements or significant data sensitivity.</p></p>
