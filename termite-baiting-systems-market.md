<p><p>Termite baiting systems are classified into three main market types based on the termite species they target. Subterranean termite baiting systems are designed to control termites that live underground, often using bait stations installed in the soil. Dampwood termite baiting systems focus on managing termites that thrive in decaying wood with high moisture levels, typically requiring bait placed in affected wood. Drywood termite baiting systems target termites that inhabit dry wood, often utilizing localized treatments to eliminate infestations effectively.</p></p>
