<p><p>The magnetic alloys market includes several key types. Steel, primarily composed of iron and carbon, is widely used for its affordability and strength, suitable for various magnetic applications. Alnico, a combination of iron, nickel, cobalt, and aluminum, is known for its excellent temperature stability and is often used in permanent magnets. Permalloy, made from iron and nickel, features high magnetic permeability and low coercivity, making it ideal for transformers and inductors. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
