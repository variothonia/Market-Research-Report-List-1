<p><p>The advanced and targeted drug delivery market encompasses various innovative systems designed to enhance therapeutic efficacy and minimize side effects. Liposomes are spherical vesicles that encapsulate drugs, improving bioavailability and targeting. Microspheres are tiny spherical particles that can control drug release profiles. Nanoparticles operate at the nanoscale to facilitate targeted delivery and cellular uptake. Emulsions consist of immiscible liquids, enabling drug solubility and stability, further aiding in the specialized delivery of therapeutic agents. These technologies optimize treatment outcomes in diverse medical fields.</p></p>
