<p><p>The mild steel channel market comprises two primary types: U channels and C channels. U channels have a U-shaped cross-section, providing strong structural support, making them ideal for construction and manufacturing applications. C channels, with their C-shaped profile, are versatile and commonly used in framing, bracing, and support structures. Both channel types are favored for their durability and weldability, catering to various industries such as construction, automotive, and infrastructure, driving demand in the steel market.</p></p>
