<p><strong>The Grease Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Meters</li><li>Electronic Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grease meter market comprises two main types: mechanical meters and electronic meters. Mechanical meters operate through mechanical components that measure grease flow based on physical displacement or gear rotation. They are typically more durable and cost-effective for simpler applications. In contrast, electronic meters utilize advanced sensors and digital displays to provide precise measurements, often including features like data logging and real-time monitoring. Electronic meters offer enhanced accuracy and are ideal for complex lubrication systems, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
