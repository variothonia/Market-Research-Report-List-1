<p><p>The professional studio headphones market is categorized into three types based on their design: closed-back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed-back headphones provide excellent isolation, making them ideal for recording environments by minimizing sound leakage. Semi-open back headphones offer a balance, allowing some external sound while still providing decent isolation, suitable for mixing. Fully-open back headphones deliver an expansive soundstage, often preferred for critical listening and mastering, as they create a more natural and immersive audio experience.</p></p>
