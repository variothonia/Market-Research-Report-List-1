<p><p>The 3D Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) system market comprises two main types: Off-line SPI systems and In-line SPI systems. Off-line SPI systems operate separate from the production line, allowing for detailed analysis of solder paste application on a batch basis, ideal for quality assurance. In contrast, In-line SPI systems are integrated within the manufacturing process, offering real-time monitoring and immediate feedback to detect defects as they occur. Both systems enhance production efficiency and product reliability in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
