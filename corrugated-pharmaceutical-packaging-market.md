<p><strong>The Corrugated Pharmaceutical Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Wall</li><li>Double-Wall</li><li>Triple-Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated pharmaceutical packaging is classified into three main types based on wall structure: single-wall, double-wall, and triple-wall. Single-wall packaging consists of a single layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two liners, providing basic protection. Double-wall packaging adds an extra layer for enhanced durability and moisture resistance, making it suitable for heavier products. Triple-wall packaging features two layers of flutes and three liners, offering maximum strength and protection, ideal for transporting sensitive pharmaceuticals and ensuring product integrity during shipping.</p></p>
