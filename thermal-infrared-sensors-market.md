<p><p>The thermal infrared sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: photon detection thermal infrared sensors and thermal detection thermal infrared sensors. Photon detection sensors operate by detecting individual photons, offering high sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for applications like spectroscopy and imaging. In contrast, thermal detection sensors measure the temperature of objects based on emitted infrared radiation, commonly used for surveillance, environmental monitoring, and industrial applications. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, including defense, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
