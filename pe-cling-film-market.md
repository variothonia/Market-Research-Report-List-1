<p><p>The PE cling film market is categorized into three main types: LDPE, LLDPE, and HDPE. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is known for its flexibility and softness, making it ideal for food packaging. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) provides enhanced strength and stretchability, catering to both food and industrial applications. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is more rigid and durable, suitable for heavier items, but less common for cling films. Each type offers distinct benefits, catering to varying packaging needs in different sectors.</p></p>
