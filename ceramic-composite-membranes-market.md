<p><p>The ceramic composite membranes market is categorized into three main types: flat-sheet membranes, pipe membranes, and others. Flat-sheet membranes are widely used in various filtration applications due to their large surface area and ease of use. Pipe membranes, designed for tubular structures, offer increased flow rates and are effective in treating larger volumes of water. Other membranes encompass specialized designs like hollow fibers and spiral-wound configurations, catering to niche applications such as wastewater treatment and gas separation.</p></p>
