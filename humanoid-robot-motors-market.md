<p><strong>The Humanoid Robot Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Brushed Motor"</li><li>"Brushless Motor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humanoid robot motors market consists of two primary types: brushed and brushless motors. Brushed motors utilize carbon brushes to transfer electricity to the rotor, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but lower efficiency and a shorter lifespan due to wear. In contrast, brushless motors eliminate brushes, providing higher efficiency, longer service life, and reduced maintenance, making them ideal for advanced humanoid robots. The choice between these motor types affects performance, application suitability, and overall operational costs in robotic systems.</p></p>
