<p><p>The high-quality PET strapping market is primarily divided into two types: embossed and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping features a textured surface, enhancing grip and reducing slippage, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. This type is often used in bundling and palletizing, providing superior tensile strength. In contrast, smooth PET strapping offers a sleek finish and is easier to unwind, making it suitable for light to medium-duty applications where aesthetics and minimal friction are essential.</p></p>
