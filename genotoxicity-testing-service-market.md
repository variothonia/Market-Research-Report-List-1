<p><p>The genotoxicity testing service market is primarily divided into two key types: In Vivo and In Vitro tests. In Vivo tests involve evaluating the genetic toxicity of substances within living organisms, providing insights into biological interactions and systemic effects. On the other hand, In Vitro tests are conducted using cellular or tissue samples outside of a living organism, enabling rapid screening and assessment of genotoxic effects in a controlled environment. Both methods are essential for ensuring safety in pharmaceuticals and chemicals.</p></p>
