Irons are versatile clubs used for mid-range shots, characterized by their smaller clubheads and varying lofts, suitable for approach shots. Putters are specialized clubs for use on the green, focusing on precision and control to facilitate accurate putting. Together, these categories cater to different aspects of the game.</p></p>
