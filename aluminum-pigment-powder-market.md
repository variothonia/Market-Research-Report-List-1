<p><ul><li>Leafing Aluminum Pigment Powder</li><li>Non-leafing Aluminum Pigment Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum pigment powder market is divided into two main types: leafing and non-leafing aluminum pigment powders. Leafing aluminum pigments have a tendency to float to the surface of paints and coatings, creating a shiny, metallic finish that is often used for decorative purposes. In contrast, non-leafing aluminum pigments remain evenly dispersed within the medium, providing a more subdued sheen and improved durability. Both types are utilized across various industries, including automotive, cosmetics, and packaging, based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
