<p><ul><li>Embedded</li><li>Tethered</li><li>Integrated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Vehicle Infotainment (IVI) market comprises three primary types: embedded, tethered, and integrated systems. Embedded IVI systems are built directly into the vehicle, offering features like navigation, media playback, and vehicle diagnostics without external connectivity. Tethered systems rely on a mobile device connected via USB or Bluetooth, allowing access to apps and features while leveraging the device’s capabilities. Integrated systems combine both embedded and tethered elements, providing seamless connectivity and enhanced user experiences through a unified interface.</p></p>
