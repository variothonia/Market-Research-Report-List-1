<p><ul><li>Main shaft</li><li>Generator shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wind Turbine Drive Shaft Market is primarily categorized into two types: the main shaft and the generator shaft. The main shaft connects the rotor and the gearbox, playing a crucial role in converting wind energy into rotational energy. In contrast, the generator shaft transmits this rotational energy from the gearbox to the generator, where it is transformed into electrical energy. Both shafts are vital for the efficiency and performance of wind turbines, impacting overall energy output and operational reliability.</p></p>
