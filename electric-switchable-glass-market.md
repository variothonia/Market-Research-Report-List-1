<p><p>The electric switchable glass market comprises three main types: organic dyes, conducting polymers, and metal oxides. Organic dyes provide dynamic color changes and are often lightweight but may have stability issues. Conducting polymers offer flexibility and suitable transparency, making them ideal for various applications. Metal oxides, known for their durability and high performance, excel in energy efficiency and temperature resistance. Together, these materials enhance the functionality of switchable glass in architecture, automotive, and display technologies.</p></p>
