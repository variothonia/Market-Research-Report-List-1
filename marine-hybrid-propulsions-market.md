<p><strong>The Marine Hybrid Propulsions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diesel-electric</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Serial Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine hybrid propulsion market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Diesel-electric** systems utilize a diesel engine to generate electricity, which powers electric motors. This setup offers flexibility and efficiency in propulsion. </p><p>2. **Parallel hybrid** configurations combine diesel engines and electric motors, allowing them to work simultaneously or independently for optimal performance and fuel efficiency. </p><p>3. **Serial hybrid** systems feature a diesel engine that solely powers an electric generator, with propulsion delivered exclusively through electric motors, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions.</p></p>
