<p><p>The semiconductor inspection systems market includes two main types: wafer inspection systems and mask inspection systems. Wafer inspection systems are designed to detect defects on semiconductor wafers during the fabrication process, ensuring high yields and reliability. Mask inspection systems focus on analyzing photomasks used in the lithography process to guarantee precision in pattern transfer. Both systems play critical roles in maintaining quality and performance in semiconductor manufacturing, essential for the advancement of integrated circuits and electronic devices.</p></p>
