<p><p>An air plasma cutter is a tool that utilizes a high-velocity jet of ionized gas, or plasma, to cut through electrically conductive materials. It is widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and metal fabrication due to its efficiency and precision. The process involves passing an electrical arc through a gas, which can include compressed air, resulting in a high-temperature plasma that melts and blows away the material.</p><p>The Air Plasma Cutter Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for metal fabrication and welding across various industries.
