<p><p>The magnetic buzzers market is segmented into two main types: active and passive magnetic buzzers. Active magnetic buzzers generate sound when an electric current passes through them, relying on an internal oscillator. They are easy to use and require minimal external components. Conversely, passive magnetic buzzers require an external audio signal or frequency to produce sound, offering greater flexibility in sound design but needing additional circuitry. Both types cater to diverse applications in consumer electronics and industrial devices.</p></p>
