<p><p>The Shape Memory Polymer (SMP) market is categorized into two main types: heat-induced and electro-induced. Heat-induced SMPs respond to temperature changes, reverting to a predetermined shape upon heating, making them ideal for applications in medical devices and smart textiles. Conversely, electro-induced SMPs utilize electrical stimulation to achieve shape recovery, enabling their use in advanced robotics and actuators. Both types are gaining traction in various industries due to their unique capabilities for innovative and adaptive designs.</p></p>
