<p><ul><li>Gas Chromatography System</li><li>Liquid Chromatography System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromatography instrumentation market encompasses two primary types: gas chromatography (GC) systems and liquid chromatography (LC) systems. GC systems separate and analyze volatile compounds using a gas carrier, making them ideal for applications in environmental testing and petrochemical analysis. In contrast, LC systems utilize liquid as the mobile phase, suitable for a broader range of substances including biomolecules and pharmaceuticals. Both systems are essential for quality control, research, and development across various industries, driving advancements in analytical techniques.</p></p>
