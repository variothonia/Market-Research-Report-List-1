<p><p>The dichroic glass market encompasses various types with unique functionalities. Thermochromic glass changes color with temperature fluctuations, providing dynamic visual effects and energy efficiency. Electrochromic glass adapts its tint in response to electrical current, offering privacy and glare control in modern architecture. Photochromatic glass responds to UV light exposure, darkening in sunlight and returning to clear indoors, enhancing comfort and aesthetic appeal. Together, these glass types are increasingly utilized in architectural, automotive, and consumer product applications.</p></p>
