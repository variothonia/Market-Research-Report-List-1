<p><ul><li>Unipolar Membrane</li><li>Bipolar Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heterogeneous ion-exchange membrane market encompasses two main types: unipolar membranes and bipolar membranes. Unipolar membranes selectively allow the passage of either cations or anions, making them ideal for applications like water purification and electrodialysis. In contrast, bipolar membranes facilitate the simultaneous transport of both types of ions, enabling processes like electrochemical synthesis and split water into hydrogen and hydroxyl ions. Both types are critical in energy conversion, waste treatment, and various biochemical applications.</p></p>
