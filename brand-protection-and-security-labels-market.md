<p><p>Brand protection and security labels play a crucial role in preventing counterfeiting and tampering. Non-residue security labels leave no evidence upon removal, making them ideal for items needing untampered guarantees. Dual layer security labels feature two distinct layers that provide enhanced protection; they reveal hidden messages or patterns when tampered with. Security tape combines adhesive properties with protective features, keeping goods secure while also offering visual indicators of tampering, thus ensuring brand integrity and consumer trust in products.</p></p>
