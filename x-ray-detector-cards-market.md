<p><p>X-ray detector cards are categorized into dual-energy, single-energy, and multi-energy types based on their energy detection capabilities. Single-energy detectors capture X-rays at one energy level, suitable for standard imaging. Dual-energy detectors simultaneously acquire images at two different energy levels, enhancing material differentiation and tissue characterization. Multi-energy detectors expand this concept further by capturing multiple energy levels, allowing for advanced imaging applications and improved diagnostic accuracy. Each type serves distinct needs in medical diagnostics and industrial testing.</p></p>
