<p><p>The skin closure surgery tissue adhesive market is primarily segmented into polyurethane-based and cyanoacrylate-based adhesives. Polyurethane-based adhesives are known for their flexibility and enhanced strength, making them suitable for dynamic areas of the body. They often promote healing and minimize scarring. In contrast, cyanoacrylate-based adhesives provide rapid bonding and are typically used for superficial wounds, offering convenience and ease of application. Both types are increasingly favored for their ability to reduce infection risks and enhance patient comfort compared to traditional sutures.</p></p>
