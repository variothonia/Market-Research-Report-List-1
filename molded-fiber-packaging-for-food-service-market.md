<p><p>Molded fiber packaging for the food service market encompasses two primary types: wood pulp-based and non-wood pulp-based materials. Wood pulp-based packaging, derived primarily from recycled paper and cardboard, offers excellent insulation and moisture resistance, making it ideal for hot and cold food items. In contrast, non-wood pulp-based options utilize alternative fibers such as agricultural residues or bagasse, providing a sustainable solution for packaging. Both types cater to the growing demand for eco-friendly, biodegradable packaging solutions in the food service industry.</p></p>
